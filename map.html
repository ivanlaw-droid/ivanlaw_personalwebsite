Featured journey: Cathedral light & quiet streets

以教堂為起點，記錄建築細節、天空和在城市中行走的安靜時刻。

This ongoing photo series focuses on how light sits on stone — from early morning blue tones to late afternoon warmth. Instead of wide tourist shots, I frame small fragments: windows, arches and the negative space between sky and towers.

Hong Kong

Everyday streets, shop signs & foggy mornings.

Cafés under flyovers, minibus lights, old shopfront typography and early-morning markets. Hong Kong is my base and the place I keep returning to for small, quiet moments.

Seoul

Neighbourhood walks between hills and alleys.

Staircases, laundry on rooftops and small galleries hidden in side streets. I tend to shoot in the in-between spaces, when the city is just waking up or almost closing down.

Tokyo / Kyoto

Trains, quiet platforms and seasonal light.

From commuter trains to temple courtyards, I focus on rhythm: repeating lines, passing shadows and how people move through public spaces without saying a word.
