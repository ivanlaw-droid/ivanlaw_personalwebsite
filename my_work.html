Charity Film 2024

Charity Short Film for Public Welfare Organisation

A commissioned short film created for a non-profit organisation, telling real stories from their service users and volunteers. The goal was simple: turn complicated social issues into something that feels human, hopeful and easy to share.

  • Co-developed the treatment with the NGO team, aligning visuals with their existing campaign message.
